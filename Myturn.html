DAVID THURMAN

I have been a fan of Bruce Springsteen and the E Street Band since 1980. I have been to over 200 shows since 1999 all over the US and around the world.

I have made hundreds of friends in my travels and I fell in love with my wife in 2008 while traveling to shows in Europe. We were in Hamburg, Milan, Paris and Copenhagen.

Every show is different and everyone should have something in life they feel this passionate about.

Some of my most special places to have seen shows are in Kansas City, Philadelphia, Foxboro and of course New Jersey. Some people dont like stadiums but I love them.
